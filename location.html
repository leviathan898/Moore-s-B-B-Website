Getting Here

Moore's is 40 miles / 80 km from Shannon airport, and about a 2 hour drive from Dublin airport. We are located on Old Dublin Road (R338), about a 1 km from Bon Secours Hospital Galway.

If you need any assistance in reaching us at our location, please feel free to contact us.

By Car

If coming from the M6, take the N6, take the R446 towards Roscam. Then take the R338 towards Galway City and you will approach us on the left after Bon Secours Hospital.

If coming through Galway City, take the N6 East and take the R338 when you reach the junction after cross River Corrib.

Bus

Bus Eireann services arrive and depart from various locations across the city.

Train

We are located 20 min walking from Galway (Ceannt) station.

Private Hire

Taxis are available on request, as is our bike rental service. We also offer a private shuttle bus for large groups; please contact us for more details.
