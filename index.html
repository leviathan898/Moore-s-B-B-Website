Our History

Based in a renovated townhouse, Moore's Bed & Breakfast is a family run B&B in operation for the past 20 years, passed down to the current owner Gillian Moore from her father. Constructed during the 1920's, the original townhouse underwent a large refurbishment in early 2012, with the interiors being renovated to give a more contemporary atmosphere while retaining a sense of history. We guarantee you will enjoy your stay, as we provide exemplary quality of service in a luxurious setting. Moore's offers spectacular views over Galway Bay on the west coast of Ireland, giving you an experience that captures the heart of the West in all it's natural beauty.
