What We Offer

All our rooms are luxuriously furnished and are non-smoking. Following renovations, the previous stone floors have been replaced with walnut wooden floors and equipped with central heating. Every room has their own ensuite private bathrooms with state‐of‐the‐art jet‐powered baths and separate walk‐in tropical rain showers.

We provide each room with an iron and ironing board (or you can partake of our in‐house services), a hair dryer, a 32" to 40" flatscreen TV and tea/coffee making facilities. Wi‐Fi is available throughout our premises.

A healthy continental breakfast is included in your stay, as we believe a good start to the day is important to all our guests. We also provide free private off street car parking, and all taxes are included in your booking. A bike hire service (including safety gear) is also available.

Single Room

Our single rooms sleeps a max. of 1 person and has a private balcony with views of the surrounding bay.

Double/Twin Room

Our double/twin rooms sleeps a max. of 2 people.

Triple Room

Our triple rooms sleeps a max. of 3 people. Three single beds or one double bed and one single bed are available.

Family Room

Our family rooms sleeps a max. of 2 adults and 2 children. One double and two single beds are available. Cots provided upon request.
