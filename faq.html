Frequently Asked Questions

General questions about your stay? Check our FAQ down below, or contact us directly.

  • Q: How do I check room availability?
  • A: Simply fill in our online form and we will get right back to you.
  • Q: How do I make a reservation?
  • A: After checking availability, simply follow the on‐screen instructions. Credit/debit card details are required for reservations.
  • Q: What time can I check‐in?
  • A: Normal check‐in time is between 11:00am ‐ 5:30pm. If you are arriving outside of these times, please arrange it with us beforehand. No check‐in can be arranged for after 9:00pm.
  • Q: What time must I check‐out by?
  • A: 11:30am.
  • Q: Is Wi‐Fi internet access available?
  • A: Yes, free Wi‐Fi is available throughout the house.
  • Q: What should I do if I need to cancel?
  • A: Inform us as soon as possible. It is not possible to refund cancellations within 3 days of arrival.
